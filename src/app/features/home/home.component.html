This site is intended for Principals of secondary school who want to issue one or more StudentCredentials to one or more students of the school.

  • The issuer of the StudentCredential must be the principal of the school. The principal will sign the StudentCredential with an digital certificate, which can be either a personal one or a certificate of representation.
  • The receiver of the Student (both the subject and holder of the credential) can be any student of the school. The principal will delegate a restricted set of power to that person that represents the different superior cycles. Those restricted power are included inside the credential and can be verified by any Relying party to whom the holder presents the StudentCredential.

You need a certificate to be able to use the system. In addition you will have to register your entity email.

Note: if you are reading this in your mobile, most probably the button will not work, unless you have installed your certificate in the mobile. You typically need to use a PC, with the certificate installed in the browser.
